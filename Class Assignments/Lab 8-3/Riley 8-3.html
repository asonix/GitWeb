Introduction

There are many great battles involving the United States that defined the direction of our country. On this website we have highlighted four, one from the American Revolution, Civil War, World War One, and World War Two. There have been many other great battles in our history but each of these highlights a difficult, but victorious achievement for the United States and helped change the course of history.
