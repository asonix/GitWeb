Planning Your Web Site

Before you start building your web site, there are a few things you should consider. A well planned web site is much more likely to attract and keep it desired audience, and mroe likely to achieve its objective.

  1. You must have an understanding of what your web site is to accomplish and what its purpose is.
  2. You must know who your target audience is and how to appeal to the needs of your target.
  3. The content for your site should be organized and revised according to the needs of your target audience.
  4. Finally, the structure of the site should be established.
